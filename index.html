About Us

Smooth Operators, is a passionate team, made up of aspiring young engineers seeking to make our way in the field of STEM. As students in our last year of high school, our goal is to design and build the best possible car, gaining invaluable experience in the process. We aim to market our team as well through online platforms such as Instagram. We chose to partake in this challenge not only because it fuels our passion for STEM, but also because it equips us with the skills and knowledge to excel in our future endeavours.
